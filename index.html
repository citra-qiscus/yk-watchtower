Understanding Cost & Budget

Est. Cost is the expected cost of a single occurrence, set when you create or edit a task. Annual Budget is the projected yearly cost, calculated automatically from Est. Cost and how often the task recurs:

Annual Budget = Est. Cost × (365 ÷ Frequency in days) Example: Rp 1,200,000 est. cost, Monthly (30 days) → 1,200,000 × (365÷30) = Rp 14,600,000/year

You can always overwrite this number manually — useful when a vendor charges a fixed annual contract price instead of a per-visit fee. One-off tasks (no recurring frequency) default to Rp 0 budget unless you set one manually.

Actual Expense is completely different — it's the real total pulled from your completion history (the "Actual Cost" you log every time you press Done). Budget is the plan; Actual Expense is what really happened.

Dashboard Insights Explained

Month-over-Month Trend
Compares the two most recent months that have any recorded expense, and shows the percentage change between them.
Projected Year-End
A run-rate forecast: (total spent so far ÷ months elapsed) × 12. Only shown for the current year — past years show their final closed total instead.
Category vs Budget
Compares each category's actual spend this year against the sum of Annual Budget for tasks in that category.
Top 5 Costliest
The 5 tasks with the highest total actual expense in the selected year, ranked highest first.
Expense Heatmap
Total actual expense grouped by Area/Equipment for the selected year, sorted highest first.

Reports

  • Overview Report — set Month filter to "Full Year", then Download Report. Annual summary with expense trends and category recap.
  • Monthly Report — pick a specific month first. Shows what was completed and what's still due that month.
  • Reports are formatted for A4 — open the downloaded file and print or "Save as PDF" from your browser.

Managing Tasks

  • Done — logs the actual date & cost. For recurring tasks, the next due date is auto-calculated from the frequency.
  • Edit — change any task detail without affecting its history.
  • History — expand to see every past completion for that task.
  • Delete — removes the task and its history permanently. This cannot be undone.

Data & Storage

All data is stored in a shared database on the server — not in your browser. That means:

  • Everyone who opens this link sees the same live data, whether on a laptop, phone, or a colleague's device.
  • Switching tabs (Overview / Task List / History) automatically pulls the latest data, so you'll see updates made by others.
  • Use Export CSV (on the Task List and History tabs) regularly for your own backups or offline sharing.
Note: this dashboard currently has no login — anyone with the link can view and edit data. Keep the link within your team.
